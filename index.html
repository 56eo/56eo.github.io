MOTIVATION

We aim to use satelite and aerial imagery to solve everyday problems for citizens, and thus improve life quality and inform about environmental risks. With our unique expertise in the fields of data analysis, remote sensing, environmental physics and mathematics we offer a broad package of solution techniques for environmental issues. For example, we started Air-Panel for analysis of pollution-data from satelite imagery to measure the effect of policies on the air quality in cities and ports. For more information on our current projects, take a look at the project explanation on our website or contact us via email with your question.
